POWS Online

POWS Online is where all the resources are kept.

It is structured into subjects, topics, lessons and activities. There are some pre-prepared activities but teachers will be able to add and share their own resources.

In order make POWS easily accessible for teachers, activities are going to be stored in an online database.

Activities are practical resources which will identify with one or more of the POWS elements. Resource will be included with each activity.

Teachers can also search by topic or POWS elements for relevant activities that can be combined together to make a lesson.

Lessons can then be saved and shared with other teachers.
