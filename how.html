POWS Online

POWS Online is a practical way to interact with the framework.

Teachers can share activities and lessons in a structured way.

The Activities

Activities are the smallest pieces of POWS Online.

They are practical resources to help teach in different ways, specifically through: Pictures, Objects, Words and Stories.

Topics and Subjects

In order to make POWS Online easily accessible, lessons and activities are also stored as subjects and topics.

The Community

Teachers can log in and add their own lessons and download other peoples lessons.
